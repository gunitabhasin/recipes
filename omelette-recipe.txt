Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 tomato
 - 1/4 onion
 - Small teaspoon of olive oil

Directions:
  1. Crack the two eggs open into a big bowl. 
  2. Chop the tomato and onion
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and onion to the bowl of eggs. Beat until light and fluffy.
  4. Grease a medium saucepan with olive oil and then add the mixture prepared in Step 3.
  5. Cook until brown and serve warm. Your omelette is ready and will taste delicious!
